Behind the Veil: Enhanced Indoor 3D Scene Reconstruction with Occluded Surfaces Completion
Su Sun, Cheng Zhao, Yuliang Guo Ruoyu Wang, Xinyu Huang, Victor(Yingjie) Chen, Liu Ren,
CVPR, 2024
Github / arXiv

Our method, for the first time, tackles the task of completing the occluded scene surfaces, resulting in a complete 3D scene mesh. The core idea of our method is to utilize the learned contextual structure prior from various scenes to infer the occluded geometry from visible depth measurements on an unseen scene.
